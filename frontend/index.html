Here's how to use the Nutrition Analyzer

✏️

How to write

Type the quantity + unit + ingredient in one input field. Multiple ingredients can be combined using "and" or "," (commas).

📏

Supported units

All common units are supported — weight, volume, or count.

g kg lbs oz cup tbsp tsp
💡

Examples

10g brisket and fries
2 lbs brisket and fries
1 cup oatmeal and banana
10 tbsp olive oil

💬 Tip: Click any example above to auto-fill the input!

⚠️

Important note on commas

If your query contains commas, replace them with "%2C" to avoid errors. Example: salt%2C pepper
